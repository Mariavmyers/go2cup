## Safety and credibility rules

- Do not claim GO2CUP is official.
- Do not claim GO2CUP is affiliated with FIFA.
- Do not claim confirmed partnerships unless confirmed.
- Do not invent affiliate links.
- Do not invent aviation, hotel, sponsor, or agency relationships.
- Remove or soften words like “official,” “guaranteed,” “confirmed,” “exclusive,” “auction,” and “waitlisted” unless verified.

## Footer rules

Add a consistent footer where possible:

GO2CUP — Independent fan-built World Cup 2026 hub | Built by Maria Myers | Powered by Estage

Required disclaimer:

GO2CUP is independent and is not affiliated with FIFA, the FIFA World Cup, host cities, stadium operators, national teams, or official sponsors unless clearly stated.

Affiliate note:

Some links may be affiliate or referral links. GO2CUP may earn a commission at no extra cost to users. Prices, availability, rules, and booking terms must be verified directly with the provider.

## Codex rules

- Do not redesign pages.
- Do not remove existing sections unless they are broken, duplicated, or legally risky.
- Do not rewrite page copy unless needed for clarity, trust, navigation, or legal safety.
- Preserve existing CSS classes where possible.
- Fix broken links.
- Standardize navigation.
- Standardize footer language.
- Use relative links for internal pages.
- Use external Estage links only for opt-ins or forms.
