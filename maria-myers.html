Maria Vernillo is a multi-disciplinary entrepreneur and strategic advisor whose work spans artificial intelligence, sports broadcasting, media production, and brand marketing. She founded MainEntry LLC to bridge the gap between cutting-edge technology and real-world business outcomes.

With fluency in English, Spanish, and Italian, Maria operates across global markets — advising organizations on how to navigate digital transformation, build authentic brand equity, and leverage AI as a competitive advantage.
