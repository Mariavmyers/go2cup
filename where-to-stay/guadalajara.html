📍 Where to Stay — Guadalajara World Cup 2026

Best Areas to Stay for Estadio Akron

Guadalajara is a sprawling city and the stadium is in Zapopan — not in the historic centre. Your choice of neighbourhood affects travel time, budget, atmosphere, and safety. These three zones cover the full range for World Cup fans.

🏟
Near Estadio Akron / Zapopan
0–5km from stadium · Walk or short taxi
Staying in Zapopan puts you closest to the action on match days. The area around the stadium has grown substantially — good hotel supply, shopping malls, and easy access. No post-match traffic chaos. For fans attending multiple games or prioritising match-day convenience above all, this is the smartest base.
Closest to Stadium
🏢
Providencia / Financial District
~12km from stadium · 20–35 min by Uber
Guadalajara's polished business and residential corridor. Upscale restaurants, cocktail bars, safe streets, and a strong hotel supply across all budgets. Well-positioned between the stadium and the historic centre — a smart middle-ground for fans who want comfort, nightlife, and reasonable match-day travel without going full Zapopan.
Best Balance
🏛
Centro Histórico / Downtown
~18km from stadium · 30–50 min by Uber
The soul of Guadalajara — Cathedral, Hospicio Cabañas, mariachi, street food, and the full cultural experience. Great for fans mixing tourism with football. Budget hotels are plentiful here. The tradeoff is distance and traffic on match days — plan Uber departures early and factor in 30–50 minutes each way during peak times.
Best Culture & Value
