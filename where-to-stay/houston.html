📍 Where to Stay — Houston World Cup 2026

Best Areas to Stay for NRG Stadium

Houston has no real public transit network — it runs on cars and rideshare. That makes your hotel location the single biggest variable in your match-day experience. These three zones cover every type of fan, from stadium-door convenience to Houston's top nightlife and business districts.

🏟
Near NRG Stadium / Medical Center
0–2 miles from stadium · Walk or short Uber
The smartest base for match-day fans. The NRG / Medical Center corridor has a strong mid-range hotel supply built for conference and medical visitors — reliable, clean, and well-priced. The Texas Medical Center is one of the world's largest, so infrastructure is solid. No traffic nightmares, no surge-pricing Ubers post-match. If you're attending multiple games, staying here is a no-brainer.
Best Match-Day Convenience
🏙
Downtown Houston
~4 miles from stadium · 10–25 min by Uber
Downtown Houston is more vibrant than its reputation suggests — Discovery Green park, the Theater District, good bars along Main Street, and a solid mix of hotels at all price points. The METRORail Red Line runs directly from Downtown to NRG Station, making it the only reliable non-rideshare option on match days. A strong all-round base for fans who want city life without Galleria-level prices.
Best City Access
🛍
Galleria / Uptown
~15 miles from stadium · 25–50 min by Uber
Houston's most upscale district — the Galleria mall, five-star hotels, Houston's best restaurant concentration, and a polished nightlife scene along Westheimer. The furthest zone from NRG Stadium, and Houston traffic on I-610 on match days is punishing. Worth it for fans prioritising luxury and dining over match-day convenience. Pre-book return rideshare before kick-off, not after. Premium & Best Dining
