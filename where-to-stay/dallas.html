📍 Best Areas to Stay

Where to Base Yourself in Dallas

AT&T Stadium is in Arlington — not downtown Dallas. That's the most important thing to understand before booking. Here are the three zones every fan should consider, ranked by proximity and convenience.

⭐ Best for Match Day
Near AT&T Stadium
Arlington
📍 0.3 – 1.5 miles to stadium

The closest hotel zone to AT&T Stadium. A handful of properties sit within a 10–20 minute walk, the rest are a short rideshare away. This is the top choice for fans who want to roll out of bed, walk to the match, and stumble back after. Inventory is extremely limited — book as early as possible.

Walkable or 5-min Uber to stadium gates
Close to Globe Life Field and Entertainment District
No parking headaches — just walk it
High demand: sells out first for match weekends
💰 Best Value
Downtown
Dallas
📍 18–22 miles · 25–35 min by car

More hotel options, better prices, and the full Dallas experience — Deep Ellum bars, Klyde Warren Park, rooftop restaurants. The trade-off is distance. You'll need to drive or rideshare to the stadium, and match-day traffic in Arlington is notoriously brutal. Plan for 60+ minutes before kickoff.

Much wider range of hotels at all price points
Nightlife, food, and entertainment all steps away
More availability even close to tournament dates
Budget to luxury — every tier represented
🏙️ Upscale Pick
Uptown
Dallas
📍 20–25 miles · 30–40 min by car

Dallas's most polished neighborhood — tree-lined streets, boutique hotels, top-tier restaurants, and a walkable nightlife strip along McKinney Avenue. Ideal for fans who want a premium base camp and don't mind the commute to Arlington. Pre-book a rideshare for match days or join an organized shuttle.

Upscale boutique and luxury hotel options
Best restaurant and nightlife density in Dallas
Safe, walkable neighborhood with great atmosphere
Easy access to DFW Airport on non-match days
