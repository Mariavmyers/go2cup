📍 Where to Stay — Mexico City World Cup 2026

Best Areas to Stay for Estadio Azteca

Mexico City is massive. Where you stay significantly affects your match-day experience, costs, and safety. These three zones cover the full range — from stadium-adjacent convenience to the city's best food and nightlife scenes.

🏟
Near Estadio Azteca (Coyoacán / Tlalpan)
0–5km from stadium · Walk or short taxi
The most practical base for match days. Coyoacán is one of Mexico City's most charming neighbourhoods — cobblestone streets, Frida Kahlo Museum, excellent street food. Tlalpan is quieter and more residential but puts you right next to the stadium. Demand here will be extreme — book the moment you secure tickets.
Closest to Stadium
🌮
Roma / Condesa
~16km from stadium · 30–50 min by Uber
CDMX's hippest neighbourhoods — tree-lined streets, world-class restaurants, rooftop bars, and a vibrant nightlife scene. Perfect if your trip is part football, part city exploration. Uber and Metro Line 9 give decent access to the stadium zone, though match-day traffic can stretch journey times significantly.
Best Nightlife & Food
💎
Polanco
~22km from stadium · 40–60 min by Uber
Mexico City's premium district — luxury hotels, designer boutiques, Michelin-calibre dining, and the safest streets in the city. Ideal for fans prioritising comfort, security, and quality. Further from the stadium, but Polanco's taxi and Uber supply is reliable even post-match. Worth every peso if budget allows.
Premium & Safest
