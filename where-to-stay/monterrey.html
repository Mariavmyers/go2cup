📍 Where to Stay — Monterrey World Cup 2026

Best Areas to Stay for Estadio BBVA

Monterrey is compact but the stadium is in Guadalupe — east of the centre. Your neighbourhood choice determines your match-day commute, safety level, nightlife access, and overall budget. These three zones cover every type of fan.

🏟
Near Estadio BBVA (Guadalupe)
0–5km from stadium · Walk or short taxi
Staying in Guadalupe puts you right next to the action. The area has seen significant hotel development since BBVA opened and offers solid mid-range options at competitive prices. No post-match traffic stress, no long Uber waits. The smart pick for fans attending multiple matches who want maximum convenience over nightlife or dining variety.
Closest to Stadium
💎
San Pedro Garza García
~15km from stadium · 20–35 min by Uber
Mexico's wealthiest municipality and Monterrey's premier upscale district. Think Polanco-level safety, luxury hotels, world-class restaurants, and manicured streets. San Pedro is the go-to base for premium fans — Uber supply is excellent, the area is well-lit and walkable at night, and the quality of hotels here is the highest in the metro area.
Premium & Safest
🏙
Monterrey Centro
~12km from stadium · 20–30 min by Uber
Downtown Monterrey — the Macroplaza, Barrio Antiguo nightlife, street food, and the best budget hotel supply in the city. Great for fans combining tourism with football. Barrio Antiguo has Monterrey's liveliest bar scene, and the Centro is well-connected. Allow extra time on match days as traffic on the main avenues builds quickly.
