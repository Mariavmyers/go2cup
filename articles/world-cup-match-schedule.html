Knockout Stage: Key Dates

After the group stage concludes on July 2, the knockout bracket begins. All times are in local stadium time zones.

  • Round of 32: July 4–7 (16 matches across multiple US cities)
  • Round of 16: July 9–12 (8 matches)
  • Quarter-finals: July 14–15 (4 matches)
  • Semi-final 1: July 18
  • Semi-final 2: July 19
  • Third-Place Play-off: July 22
  • The Final: July 19, MetLife Stadium, New York/New Jersey

Travel tip: The knockout stage brings rapidly changing travel needs. Book refundable accommodation in Final-stage cities (New York area for the Final, major hubs for semis) and cancel if your team does not advance. Flights between host cities book up fast after each round — monitor results and book within 24 hours of knowing where your team plays next.

For venue details and neighbourhood guides, visit the host cities overview, the Mexico City host city page or the New York / New Jersey guide for the Final.
