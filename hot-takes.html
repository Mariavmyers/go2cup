Updated · July 4, 2026 · Round of 32

Who wins it? Here's my honest read.

After the Round of 32, I'm changing my pick: France lift this trophy. They're not the flashiest, but they're quietly the deepest — they beat Paraguay when they had to and they don't beat themselves. That's the profile that survives knockout football. Spain look ruthless and Brazil's attack is humming, but the real story of this round was the scares: Argentina got made to sweat like a pig by Cape Verde, and Morocco dumped co-host Canada 3–0 to prove last cycle was no fluke. Wide open at the top — but France is the one I'd trust to be standing on July 19.
